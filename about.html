More About Me.

I have a Bsc in Geology (2012) and a MSc in Geotectonics (2015), both at the Institute of Geosciences of the University of São Paulo, Brazil. I was Temporary Professor between 2016 and 2018 at IGc-USP.

I’m a PhD student at IGc-USP (Mineral Resources). I'm also an undergraduate student in Computer Engineerig at UNIVESP (Virtual University of São Paulo State).

My research interests include Photogrammetry, UAVs, LiDAR, Remote Sensing, Free Software (FOSS), DEMs, GIS, Structural Geology, Rock Mechanics and Rock Engineering.

I am working as a geologist at the Civil Defense of São Paulo city (COMDEC - Divisão de Prevenção).
